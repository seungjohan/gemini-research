# Product Manager @ Bear Robotics (Seoul)

## 📋 Role Overview
Bear Robotics is seeking a **Product Manager** with 3–10 years of experience to lead the end-to-end lifecycle of their AI-based mobility platforms. Based in Seoul, this role involves defining the next generation of robot-human interaction and operational excellence.

## 🛠️ Key Responsibilities
- **Product Lifecycle:** Plan and improve features for robots and services from research to launch.
- **Data-Driven UX:** Define problems using field data and user feedback.
- **Documentation:** Author PRDs, flowcharts, and user stories for cross-functional alignment.
- **AI Automation:** Utilize AI tools to accelerate team execution and automate workflows.

## ✅ Qualifications
- **Minimum:** 3+ years in PM/Service Planning, experience launching mobile apps or robot products, proficiency in English (business level).
- **Preferred:** 0→1 service launch experience, active use of AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.), understanding of robotics/IoT/Logistics.

## 🎁 Benefits & Culture
- **Financial:** RSUs, performance incentives, meal allowance.
- **Work Style:** Flexible hours (core start 8-11 AM), high-spec equipment (4K monitors).
- **Growth:** English education support, comprehensive health check-ups.
