# Product Release Manager @ Wayve

## 📋 Role Overview
Wayve is looking for a **Product Release Manager** to manage the software release lifecycle for their autonomous driving systems. Based in London, this role ensures that their Embodied AI platforms are deployed safely and consistently to their fleet and partner OEMs.

## 🛠️ Key Responsibilities
- **Release Lifecycle:** Establish and manage the end-to-end release process, including cadence, documentation, and testing.
- **Safety Compliance:** Ensure all software releases comply with automotive safety standards like ISO 26262, SOTIF, and ASPICE.
- **Change Management:** Lead the Change Control Board (CCB) to prioritize and approve system changes.
- **Cross-functional Coordination:** Synchronize efforts between Engineering, Safety, and Product teams to ensure mission-ready deployments.

## ✅ Qualifications
- **Experience:** 10+ years in software release management, ideally in automotive or safety-critical sectors.
- **Technical Knowledge:** Deep understanding of ADAS/AV embedded software and JIRA/JAMA workflows.
- **Standards Mastery:** Proven expertise in automotive functional safety and software quality standards.
