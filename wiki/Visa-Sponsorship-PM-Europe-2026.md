> [!IMPORTANT] Key Takeaway
> **Why this matters:** The European tech market in 2026 is defined by a fierce competition for AI and Product talent. While Big Tech remains stable, high-growth startups and mid-sized scale-ups offer the most dynamic visa-friendly environments for PMs.
> **How to use it:** Prioritize "Recognized Sponsors" (NL), "A-rated Sponsors" (UK), or "Innovative Companies" (FR) to bypass local labor market tests. Focus on "Technical PM - AI" roles for the highest sponsorship priority.
> **Informs:** International job search strategy, relocation planning, and compensation benchmarking for 0→1 Product Managers.
