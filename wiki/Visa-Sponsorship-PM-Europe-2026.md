> [!IMPORTANT] Key Takeaway
> **Why this matters:** The European tech market in 2026 is defined by a fierce competition for AI and Product talent, leading many Tier 1 and Tier 2 cities to streamline visa sponsorship for PMs.
> **How to use it:** Focus on "Recognized Sponsors" (UK/NL) or companies eligible for the "EU Blue Card" (DE/FR) to maximize success. Specialized AI roles (Technical PM - AI) have the highest sponsorship approval rates.
> **Informs:** International job search strategy and relocation planning for 0→1 Product Managers.

# Visa Sponsorship Companies for Product Managers (Europe 2026)

This list identifies IT and AI-focused companies in key European hubs that are known to sponsor working visas for non-EU/EEA nationals, specifically for Product Management positions.

## 🇮🇪 Ireland (Dublin)
Dublin serves as the EMEA headquarters for many US tech giants. The primary route is the **Critical Skills Employment Permit**.

### AI & Big Tech
- **Google Ireland:** Active sponsor for PM and Product Operations roles in "Silicon Docks."
- **Amazon / AWS:** Frequently sponsors for cloud infrastructure and AI product management.
- **Meta:** Sponsoring for technical program management and AI infrastructure.
- **Salesforce:** Actively hires for AI-driven enterprise platform PM roles.
- **Workday:** A major local employer with a strong history of sponsoring international PMs.
- **TikTok:** Actively growing its European HQ with various PM and operations roles.

### IT & Consulting
- **Version 1:** Leading Irish IT services firm that sponsors AI solution and product roles.
- **Accenture:** Top sponsor for technology and transformation consulting PMs.
- **Intercom:** Dublin-born AI-first customer service platform that frequently sponsors.

---

## 🇬🇧 United Kingdom (London)
London is the leading hub for AI investment in Europe. The primary route is the **Skilled Worker Visa**.

### AI Foundational & Infrastructure
- **Mistral AI (EMEA HQ):** Actively recruiting PMs for foundational model integration.
- **Google DeepMind:** The global center for AI research and productization.
- **Anthropic:** Expanding its London presence for AI safety and product roles.
- **Proton:** Privacy-first AI company (HQ in Geneva, large London presence).

### Fintech & Scale-ups
- **Revolut:** One of the most prolific sponsors for PMs at all levels.
- **Monzo:** Regularly hires and sponsors for product squads.
- **Wise:** Known for a streamlined internal visa sponsorship process.
- **Checkout.com:** Major B2B payments firm that actively recruits international PMs.
- **Palantir:** Hires "Forward Deployed Product Managers" with full sponsorship.

---

## 🇩🇪 Germany (Berlin)
Germany faces a significant labor shortage in 2026, making it one of the most visa-friendly countries. The **EU Blue Card** is the standard for PMs.

### Fintech & E-commerce
- **N26:** Has a dedicated "Global Mobility" team for PM sponsorship.
- **Zalando:** Europe’s leading fashion platform with a very structured relocation process.
- **Klarna:** Frequently sponsors international talent for its "AI-powered" shopping tools.
- **SumUp:** Global payments company hiring PMs for hardware and software with full support.

### Mobility & AI Logistics
- **Tesla (Giga Berlin):** Constant recruiter for AI-integrated manufacturing and product roles.
- **Delivery Hero:** Global food delivery giant with a highly international workforce.
- **HelloFresh:** Sponsors PMs for digital product and supply chain optimization.
- **GetYourGuide:** Offers comprehensive relocation and visa assistance.

---

## 🇳🇱 Netherlands (Amsterdam)
Amsterdam offers the **Highly Skilled Migrant** program, one of the fastest visa processes in Europe.

### High-Growth Tech
- **Adyen:** Scaling AI-driven personalization and fraud detection teams.
- **Booking.com:** One of the largest tech employers in Amsterdam with robust sponsorship.
- **ASML:** While based in Veldhoven, has a large Amsterdam presence for technical PMs.
- **Miro:** The visual collaboration platform; highly international and visa-friendly.
- **Pleo:** Fintech scale-up that actively sponsors for product roles.

---

## 🇫🇷 France (Paris)
Paris is a rising AI hub, supported by the **Talent Passport (Passeport Talent)**.

### AI Hub
- **Mistral AI:** The primary target for PMs interested in LLM productization.
- **Hugging Face:** While remote-friendly, maintains a strong Paris presence with sponsorship.
- **Doctolib:** Integrating AI into healthcare; actively sponsors for product leadership.
- **Contentsquare:** AI-powered UX analytics firm with global sponsorship capabilities.

---

## 🇪🇸 Spain (Madrid)
The **Startup Law (Ley de Startups)** has streamlined sponsorship for "Highly Skilled Professionals."

### Mobility & ESG AI
- **Cabify:** Mobility AI unicorn that actively sponsors international PMs.
- **Clarity AI:** Leading Sustainability/ESG AI platform with a global team.
- **Glovo:** Part of Delivery Hero; a major recruiter for international product talent.
- **FeverUp:** Fast-growing entertainment tech unicorn that sponsors for product roles.

---

## 🇨🇭 Switzerland (Geneva)
High salaries but restrictive quotas. Sponsorship usually requires senior experience.

### Privacy & Fintech AI
- **Proton:** The most active sponsor in Geneva for privacy-focused AI products.
- **SonarSource:** AI for Code; frequently sponsors senior technical PMs.
- **Swissquote:** Leader in Fintech AI with a history of international hiring.
- **Avaloq:** Sponsoring for specialized wealth-tech product management.

### AI & Fintech

- **Feedzai:** Financial AI and fraud prevention; established history of international hiring.
- **Defined.ai:** AI data platform; actively recruits global product talent.
- **Talkdesk:** Cloud contact center with AI integration; highly visa-friendly.
