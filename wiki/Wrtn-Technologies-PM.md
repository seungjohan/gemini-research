# Product Manager (B2C) @ Wrtn Technologies

## 📋 Role Overview
Wrtn Technologies is looking for a **Product Manager** to drive growth and engagement for their leading B2C AI platform. This role focuses on defining business hypotheses, testing them rapidly, and managing the product lifecycle for services like the AI character platform.

## 🛠️ Key Responsibilities
- **Growth Strategy:** Design and validate business hypotheses to drive service growth.
- **Product Lifecycle:** Lead the entire process from planning to launch and operation in collaboration with engineering and design squads.
- **KPI Management:** Measure performance using data and intuition to achieve growth targets.
- **Process Design:** Author PRDs and design efficient operational workflows.

## ✅ Qualifications
- **Experience:** 3+ years in service planning and development collaboration.
- **Critical Thinking:** Strong logical thinking and fast decision-making skills.
- **Preferred:** Experience in content-based platforms or understanding of AI character tech.
