# Solar Product Manager @ Upstage

## 📋 Role Overview
Upstage is seeking a **Solar Product Manager** to define the technical strategy and roadmap for their Solar LLM (Large Language Model) and VLM (Vision Language Model) series. This role is central to Upstage's mission of delivering "Sovereign AI" for enterprises.

## 🛠️ Key Responsibilities
- **Model Strategy:** Define the development direction and roadmap for Solar LLMs and VLMs.
- **End-to-End Leadership:** Collaborate with research and business teams to move models from labs to market.
- **Performance Evaluation:** Manage benchmarks and processes to ensure world-class model performance.
- **Operational Systems:** Build automated systems for project monitoring and deployment.

## ✅ Qualifications
- **Experience:** 5+ years in IT product planning or project leadership.
- **Technical Depth:** Deep understanding of AI/ML technologies and product development cycles.
- **Ownership:** Strong ability to resolve technical blockers and lead complex cross-functional projects.
