# Sr. Product Manager @ MarqVision

## 📋 Role Overview
MarqVision is seeking a **Senior Product Manager** to lead the development of their AI-powered brand protection solutions. This role focuses on building mission-critical tools that detect and combat intellectual property infringement across global online marketplaces.

## 🛠️ Key Responsibilities
- **AI Enforcement Engine:** Build and scale automated tools for detecting and enforcing IP rights (trademarks, copyrights, designs).
- **Technical Strategy:** Translate complex legal and brand requirements into technical requirements for Vision AI and automation squads.
- **Product Roadmap:** Own the strategy and OKRs for the Brand Protection product line.
- **Global Collaboration:** Work across offices in NY, SF, and Seoul to ensure product-market fit in diverse global markets.

## ✅ Qualifications
- **Experience:** 5+ years of technical PM experience in B2B SaaS.
- **Domain Interest:** Deep interest in IP law, brand integrity, and anti-counterfeiting technologies.
- **Skills:** Strong analytical thinking and ability to work with Computer Vision (CV) models.
- **Bonus:** Bilingual proficiency in English and Korean.
