# Principal Product Manager @ Synthesia

## 📋 Role Overview
Synthesia is seeking a **Principal Product Manager** to lead the vision for their core video creation and editing experience. Based in London, this role is about making professional-grade video production accessible to everyone through AI.

## 🛠️ Key Responsibilities
- **Product Vision:** Lead the roadmap for the Synthesia Editor, Player, and public APIs.
- **UX Innovation:** Simplify complex editing workflows for non-experts while maintaining power for pros.
- **Enterprise Scale:** Ensure the platform meets the needs of global enterprise clients.
- **Research-to-Product:** Bridge the gap between generative AI research and user-facing features.

## ✅ Qualifications
- **Experience:** 7+ years in PM, specifically with creative tools or complex B2B platforms.
- **AI Intuition:** Deep interest in generative AI and its impact on creative workflows.
- **Leadership:** Proven ability to lead cross-functional squads (Eng, Design, Research).
