# Product Manager @ Rogo AI

## 📋 Role Overview
Rogo is seeking a **Product Manager** to help build the first autonomous AI analyst for the financial industry. Based on-site in New York, this role involves owning feature development from discovery to demo, specifically focusing on automating complex investment workflows.

## 🛠️ Key Responsibilities
- **Feature Development:** Lead the development of new AI-driven features for memos, models, and presentation decks.
- **LLM Design:** Design and iterate on complex LLM prompts and behaviors to ensure accuracy and usefulness for financial pros.
- **Sales & GTM:** Participate in sales calls and demos with hedge funds and PE firms to gather feedback and drive adoption.
- **Enterprise Security:** Partner with engineering to design admin and security tools that meet the strict compliance needs of global banks.

## ✅ Qualifications
- **Hybrid Background:** 1–2 years in investment banking or investing + 1–2 years in PM or a high-growth startup.
- **Technical Literacy:** Deep intuition for LLM prompting and a technical degree (CS/Math/Physics/Engineering).
- **Communication:** Exceptional ability to demo complex technical products to sophisticated financial audiences.
