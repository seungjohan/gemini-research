# Product Manager @ Hebbia

## 📋 Role Overview
Hebbia is seeking a **Product Manager** to help build the reasoning engine for the world's most knowledge-intensive industries. Based on-site in New York (SoHo), this role involves owning the full product lifecycle and driving the technical strategy for AI-native applications.

## 🛠️ Key Responsibilities
- **Full Lifecycle Ownership:** Drive product development from initial scoping and technical discovery to launch and iteration.
- **Technical Execution:** Get hands-on with prompt engineering, LLM optimization, and technical architecture in collaboration with engineering.
- **Leadership Partnership:** Partner directly with the CEO and Head of Product to shape the long-term vision of the "Hebbia Matrix" platform.
- **User Discovery:** Conduct deep research with users in finance and legal sectors to identify high-value agentic workflows.

## ✅ Qualifications
- **Experience:** 3+ years in product management, ideally at an early-stage B2B SaaS startup.
- **AI Literacy:** Technical fluency in LLM behaviors, limitations, and optimization techniques.
- **Mindset:** Extreme ownership, biased toward action, and comfortable with a 5-day on-site culture.
