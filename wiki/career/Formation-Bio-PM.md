# Product Area Lead @ Formation Bio

## 📋 Role Overview
Formation Bio is looking for a **Product Area Lead** to own a major business domain (e.g., Clinical Trials, Regulatory) and drive the adoption of AI-native workflows. This leadership role is responsible for setting the strategy that accelerates drug development through technology.

## 🛠️ Key Responsibilities
- **Domain Strategy:** Define the product vision and roadmap for a critical pharma business unit.
- **AI-Native Workflows:** Lead the design and implementation of agentic AI systems that automate manual pharma operations.
- **Cross-functional Alignment:** Drive collaboration between Engineering, Data Science, and Clinical Operations teams.
- **Regulatory Compliance:** Ensure all technology solutions adhere to strict pharma regulatory standards (GxP).

## ✅ Qualifications
- **Leadership:** Proven experience leading product teams in complex, high-stakes industries.
- **Technical Judgment:** Strong ability to evaluate AI/ML feasibility for specific business outcomes.
- **Pharma/Bio Awareness:** Understanding of the drug development lifecycle and the unique constraints of the biotech sector.
