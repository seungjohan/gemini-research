# Innovation Product Manager @ Harvey AI

## 📋 Role Overview
Harvey is seeking an **Innovation Product Manager** to work on the frontlines with elite law firms and professional services organizations. Based on-site in New York, this role focuses on translating high-stakes client needs into the core product roadmap.

## 🛠️ Key Responsibilities
- **Workflow Diagnosis:** Partner with clients to identify mission-critical pain points and design AI-powered solutions.
- **Pilot to Rollout:** Lead the end-to-end lifecycle of new feature pilots, from initial discovery to firm-wide deployment.
- **Roadmap Synthesis:** Translate client feedback and market trends into actionable requirements for the core product and engineering teams.
- **AI Evangelism:** Navigate complex conversations around data privacy, governance, and the strategic value of generative AI in legal practice.

## ✅ Qualifications
- **Background:** Experience in a top-tier law firm, consulting firm, or high-growth legal tech startup.
- **Technical Literacy:** Strong understanding of generative AI/LLM capabilities and their practical applications.
- **Communication:** Exceptional ability to manage senior stakeholders and bridge the gap between technical and non-technical audiences.
