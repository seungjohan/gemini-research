# Lead Product Manager @ Twelve Labs

## 📋 Role Overview
Twelve Labs is looking for a **Lead Product Manager** to spearhead the development of AI agents and applications built on their world-leading video understanding engine. This role bridges the gap between core research and global market entry (GTM).

## 🛠️ Key Responsibilities
- **Video AI Strategy:** Define the product roadmap for video agents and developer-facing applications.
- **Data Operations:** Lead requirements for dataset definition and design automated data pipelines with research teams.
- **Cross-Functional Leadership:** Act as the glue between Research, Engineering, and GTM teams.
- **Partner Management:** Manage external vendors for data labeling and quality assurance.

## ✅ Qualifications
- **Experience:** 5+ years in AI/ML product development and launch.
- **Domain Knowledge:** Deep interest and understanding of unstructured data (video, language) and multimodal AI.
- **Analytical Depth:** Ability to translate complex model capabilities into clear product guidelines.
