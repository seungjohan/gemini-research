# Product Owner (Zeta) @ Scatter Lab

## 📋 Role Overview
Scatter Lab is seeking a **Product Owner** to lead their next-generation AI entertainment service, **Zeta**. This role is responsible for the entire product lifecycle within a cross-functional squad, focusing on rapid experimentation and user-generated content (UGC) ecosystems.

## 🛠️ Key Responsibilities
- **Strategic Definition:** Set 1–2 month solving goals and key metrics for the squad.
- **Rapid Iteration:** Select and prioritize features in 3-day to 2-week cycles.
- **Full-stack Planning:** Manage everything from high-level value definition to detailed wireframing, QA, and post-launch analysis.
- **Ecosystem Growth:** Build strategies to activate a user-led character and story creation ecosystem.

## ✅ Qualifications
- **Experience:** 3+ years in product management or service planning.
- **AI UX:** Ability to design novel user experiences using generative AI technologies.
- **Mindset:** Strong ownership and comfort with rapid, iterative development.
